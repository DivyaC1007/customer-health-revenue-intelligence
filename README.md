# Customer Health Analytics for Subscription Products
## Business Challenge

Subscription businesses need visibility into customer health, product adoption, churn risk and revenue performance.

This project simulates Salesforce CRM data and demonstrates how SQL and Power BI can be used to identify revenue opportunities, customer retention risks and executive-level business insights.

## Objectives

- Measure Customer Health
- Identify Churn Risk
- Analyze Product Adoption
- Monitor Revenue Performance
- Track Sales Pipeline

## Tools Used

- SQL
- MySQL
- Power BI
- Excel

## KPIs

- Customer Health Score
- Revenue at Risk
- Product Adoption Rate
- Win Rate
- Renewal Rate
- ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue)
