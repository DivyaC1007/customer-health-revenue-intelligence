# Customer Health & Revenue Intelligence Platform

## Business Challenge

A subscription-based intelligence company wants to improve customer retention, increase renewals, identify upsell opportunities, and gain visibility into product adoption and sales pipeline performance.

The leadership team lacks a unified view of customer health and needs actionable insights to reduce churn and maximize revenue growth.

## Stakeholders

* Sales Leadership
* Customer Success Team
* Product Team
* Executive Leadership

## Project Objectives

* Measure Customer Health
* Identify Churn Risk
* Analyze Product Adoption
* Monitor Revenue Performance
* Track Sales Pipeline
* Highlight Upsell Opportunities

## Business Questions

1. Which customers are most likely to churn?
2. Which products have the highest adoption rates?
3. Which accounts present the strongest upsell opportunities?
4. What revenue is currently at risk?
5. Which sales representatives generate the highest-value opportunities?
6. How effective is the current sales pipeline?

## Data Sources

This project simulates Salesforce CRM data using the following datasets:

* Accounts
* Opportunities
* Product Usage
* Support Tickets
* Renewals

## Tools Used

* SQL
* MySQL
* Power BI
* Excel

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Customer Health Score
* Churn Risk Score
* Revenue at Risk
* Product Adoption Rate
* Renewal Rate
* Win Rate
* Average Deal Size
* Pipeline Value

## Expected Outcomes

The solution provides business stakeholders with a unified view of customer health, revenue performance, product adoption, and churn risk, enabling data-driven decisions that improve customer retention and revenue growth.
